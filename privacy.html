Privacy Policy

1. Explanation of Terms

To help you understand this policy, we explain some terms below:

  • Account Information: Your name and email that you provide when signing up on our site.
  • Authentication: The process we use to check that you are who you say you are, usually when you log in.
  • Payment Processor: A company (like Razorpay) that handles payment transactions securely on our behalf.
  • Anonymized Data: Data that cannot be used to identify you personally. For example, we might use technical error reports that do not show your name or email.
  • Cookies: Small files stored on your device to help websites function properly. We only use essential cookies that are needed for the site's basic features.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information:
    When you register, we collect your name and email address for authentication.
  • Payment Information:
    Payments are securely processed via Razorpay. We do not collect or store your financial or card details.
  • Technical Information:
    We use error monitoring to collect anonymized, non-identifiable technical data only.

3. Use of Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your user account.
  • Communicate with you regarding your purchases or account.
  • Process payments and deliver digital content.
  • Diagnose and maintain website stability.
  • Provide customer support.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services to operate our website and deliver our products:

  • Supabase: Provides secure user authentication and data management for accounts.
  • Razorpay: Handles payment processing. We do not receive or store your financial information; it is handled directly by Razorpay.
  • Resend: Sends transactional and essential emails (such as account notifications and receipts) on our behalf.
  • Sentry: Monitors and diagnoses website errors using only anonymized, non-identifiable technical data.

These services may process your information as needed to provide their functions. Each third-party service has its own privacy policy, and we encourage you to review them for more details on their data practices.
